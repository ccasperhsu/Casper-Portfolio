About

When ChatGPT was first released, I was an undergrad at UCLA testing to see if it could be a substitute teacher for running regressions in my econometric course. While debates about whether we’ve reached Artificial General Intelligence continue, the surge in data-center construction—and its consequences on national electricity demand—has significant implications.

As someone who depends on reliable power, I'm interested in how rising electricity consumption affects carbon emissions and how we can meet growing demand without jeopardizing the health of our blue planet. I hope to contribute to this challenge by applying my skills in rigorous data analysis and creating visualizations that make complex information accessible.
